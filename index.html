No. This is purely educational. We cover FEGLI, TSP, and FEHB in plain language so you can make informed decisions. We run live sessions every week. Once you register, you'll receive a confirmation email with the exact date, time, and join link for the next available session. Sessions typically last 60 minutes.

Yes. This webinar is designed specifically for federal employees who have FEGLI, TSP, and FEHB benefits. The content is tailored to people with these specific federal benefit programs.

Yes. These are live, interactive sessions — not pre-recorded videos. We dedicate time at the end of each session for Q&A. If you have a specific question about your situation, our specialists will do their best to address it during the session.
