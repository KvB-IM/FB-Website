Common Questions

FAQs

Is this really free?

Yes, 100% free. There is no cost, no credit card required, and no hidden fees. Federal Benefits Exchange provides this as an educational service to help federal employees understand the benefits they've earned.

Is this a sales presentation?

No. This is an educational webinar. We cover TSP, FEGLI, FERS, FEHB, and Social Security in plain language so you can make informed decisions. At the end, attendees are informed about our optional one-on-one benefit review service — but there is no pressure, and many attendees simply attend, learn, and leave with the information they needed.

Is Federal Benefits Exchange affiliated with the government?

No. Federal Benefits Exchange is an independent education organization. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to TSP, OPM, USPS, or any government agency. All educational materials and services are provided for informational purposes only.

Will I receive individualized advice during the webinar?

The webinar is educational and covers general federal benefit concepts. It does not provide individualized investment, tax, legal, or retirement advice. After the webinar, you may request a separate, optional one-on-one benefits review where your specific situation can be discussed.

Can I ask questions during the webinar?

Yes. These are live, interactive sessions — not pre-recorded videos. We dedicate time at the end of each session for Q&A. General questions about how federal benefit programs work are welcome.

What if I can't make it?

No problem. We run this every week. If you miss your session, simply reply to your confirmation email or call us and we'll get you on the next available one. We want you to actually attend — so we'll work around your schedule.
